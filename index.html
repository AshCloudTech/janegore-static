What I Do

Counselling & Therapy Services

I offer a wide range of services to meet your personal needs

Adult Individual Therapy

Offers a supportive and confidential space to explore personal challenges, gain insight, and develop effective coping strategies. Tailored to your unique needs, it helps you navigate life’s difficulties, improve emotional well-being, and foster personal growth.

Couples Therapy

Provides a safe and supportive space to improve communication, strengthen emotional connections, and resolve conflicts. Whether you're facing challenges or seeking to deepen your relationship, therapy helps you build a healthier and more fulfilling partnership.

Adolescent Therapy

Provides a supportive and non-judgmental space for young people to explore their emotions, build resilience, and navigate life’s challenges. Through tailored support, it helps teens develop healthy coping strategies, improve self-esteem, and strengthen relationships.

Psychosexual Therapy

Offers a safe and confidential space to explore and address sexual concerns, relationship issues, and intimacy challenges. Using a tailored approach, it helps individuals and couples understand their sexual well-being, overcome difficulties, and enhance their relationships.
