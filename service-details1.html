Individual Therapy & Adult Counselling Online

  • Professional, confidential therapy for adults seeking support with anxiety, depression, trauma, relationships, and personal growth. BACP registered therapist providing expert individual counselling across the UK through secure online sessions.

Why Choose Individual Therapy for Your Mental Health?

Life presents us all with challenges that can feel overwhelming, isolating, and impossible to navigate alone. Whether you're experiencing anxiety that interferes with daily life, depression that clouds your perspective, relationship difficulties that cause ongoing stress, or trauma that continues to impact your wellbeing, individual therapy provides a safe, confidential space to explore these experiences and develop healthier ways of coping.

As a BACP registered therapist with extensive experience in adult counselling, I understand that seeking therapy represents a courageous step towards improving your mental health and overall quality of life. Individual therapy isn't about receiving advice or being told what to do - it's a collaborative process where we work together to help you understand yourself more deeply, identify patterns that may be holding you back, and develop the tools and insights necessary for meaningful, lasting change.

The Power of Individual Therapeutic Work

Individual therapy offers a unique opportunity to focus entirely on your personal experiences, goals, and healing journey without the distractions or considerations that come with group or couples therapy. This personalised approach allows for deeper exploration and more targeted therapeutic interventions.

Understanding the Benefits of Professional Counselling

Research consistently demonstrates that individual therapy can be highly effective for treating a wide range of mental health conditions and life challenges. The therapeutic relationship itself - built on trust, empathy, and professional expertise - becomes a powerful catalyst for healing and personal growth.

Many people find that therapy provides them with their first experience of being truly heard and understood without judgement. This experience alone can be profoundly healing, particularly for those who have felt misunderstood or isolated in their struggles.

Common Reasons Adults Seek Individual Therapy

People come to individual therapy for numerous reasons, often when life's challenges begin to feel unmanageable or when they recognise patterns that no longer serve them. Understanding that you're not alone in these experiences can be the first step towards healing.

Anxiety and Stress Management

Comprehensive support for generalised anxiety, panic attacks, social anxiety, work-related stress, and overwhelming worry that interferes with daily functioning and quality of life.

Depression and Mood Disorders

Professional treatment for persistent low mood, hopelessness, loss of interest in activities, emotional numbness, and the complex feelings that accompany depressive episodes.

Trauma and PTSD Recovery

Specialised trauma-informed therapy for processing difficult experiences, reducing trauma symptoms, and developing healthy coping mechanisms for moving forward.

Identity and Self-Discovery

Exploring questions about identity, values, life purpose, and personal meaning, particularly during periods of uncertainty or significant life changes.

Relationship and Communication Issues

Individual work on relationship patterns, communication skills, boundary setting, and understanding how your past experiences impact current relationships.

Low Self-Esteem and Confidence

Building healthy self-worth, challenging negative self-talk, developing self-compassion, and creating a more balanced, realistic self-perception.

Grief and Loss Support

Compassionate support through bereavement, job loss, relationship endings, health changes, or any significant life transition that involves grief and adjustment.

Life Transitions and Personal Growth

Support during major life changes such as career transitions, parenthood, retirement, or any period of significant personal development and self-discovery.

Signs You Might Benefit from Individual Therapy:

  • Persistent feelings of sadness, anxiety, or overwhelm that don't improve on their own

  • Difficulty coping with daily stressors or major life changes

  • Relationship patterns that feel unhealthy or unfulfilling

  • Sleep disturbances, appetite changes, or other physical symptoms of stress

  • Feeling stuck in negative thought patterns or behaviours

  • Loss of interest in activities you previously enjoyed

  • Difficulty making decisions or feeling confused about life direction

  • Past experiences that continue to impact your present life

My Integrative Approach to Individual Therapy

As an integratively trained therapist, I don't believe in a one-size-fits-all approach to mental health treatment. Every individual brings unique experiences, challenges, strengths, and goals to therapy. My approach involves carefully selecting and combining therapeutic techniques from various evidence-based modalities to create a personalised treatment plan that addresses your specific needs and preferences.

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)

Helping you identify and change unhelpful thought patterns and behaviours that contribute to emotional distress, anxiety, and depression. CBT provides practical tools for managing difficult situations and emotions.

Person-Centred Counselling

Creating a warm, empathetic, non-judgmental environment where you feel completely accepted and understood, allowing your natural capacity for growth and healing to emerge.

Psychodynamic Therapy

Exploring how past experiences and unconscious patterns influence your current thoughts, feelings, and behaviours, leading to deeper self-understanding and lasting change.

Mindfulness-Based Approaches

Incorporating mindfulness techniques to help you develop present-moment awareness, reduce anxiety, manage difficult emotions, and build resilience in facing life's challenges.

Solution-Focused Therapy

Concentrating on your strengths, resources, and successful strategies while working collaboratively to identify and achieve specific, realistic goals for positive change.

Trauma-Informed Practice

Understanding how trauma affects the mind and body, creating safety in the therapeutic relationship, and using specialised techniques to process traumatic experiences safely.

The Therapeutic Relationship: Foundation of Healing Work

Research consistently shows that the quality of the therapeutic relationship is one of the most important factors in successful therapy outcomes. I prioritise creating a warm, genuine, and trustworthy therapeutic alliance where you feel completely safe to explore your deepest concerns without fear of judgement.

This relationship becomes a secure base from which you can examine difficult experiences, try new ways of thinking and behaving, and gradually build confidence in your ability to handle life's challenges. Many clients find that the therapeutic relationship itself becomes a model for healthier relationships in other areas of their lives.

What to Expect from Online Individual Therapy

Getting Started: Your First Session

Beginning therapy can feel daunting, and it's completely normal to feel nervous about your first session. I understand that taking this step requires courage, and I aim to make the process as comfortable and welcoming as possible. During our initial session, we'll spend time getting to know each other, discussing what brought you to therapy, and beginning to understand your goals and expectations. I'll explain how therapy works, discuss confidentiality, and answer any questions you might have about the process. There's no pressure to share more than you're comfortable with initially - therapy is a gradual process that unfolds at your own pace.

What Happens in Your First Individual Therapy Session:

  • Comprehensive discussion about what's brought you to therapy

  • Exploration of your current challenges and how they're affecting your life

  • Initial assessment of your mental health and wellbeing

  • Discussion of your goals and what you hope to achieve through therapy

  • Explanation of my therapeutic approach and how we'll work together

  • Opportunity for you to ask questions about the therapy process

  • Beginning to build rapport and establish a therapeutic relationship

The Ongoing Therapeutic Process

Individual therapy is a collaborative journey that typically unfolds over several sessions. Each session builds upon the previous one, allowing for deeper exploration and understanding as trust develops between us. Some sessions might focus on current challenges and coping strategies, while others might explore patterns from your past that continue to influence your present experiences.

Progress in therapy isn't always linear - there may be breakthroughs, setbacks, and periods of steady growth. This is completely normal and part of the healing process. I'll help you recognise and celebrate progress, even when it feels small, whilst providing support during more challenging periods.

Benefits of Online Therapy Sessions

Online individual therapy offers numerous advantages that make mental health support more accessible and convenient. Many clients find they can be more open and relaxed when they're in their own familiar environment, leading to more productive therapeutic work.

Accessibility and Convenience

No travel time or transportation concerns, making it easier to fit therapy into your schedule. Particularly beneficial for those with mobility issues, busy lifestyles, or living in remote areas.

Comfort and Privacy

Attend sessions from your own home where you feel most comfortable and secure. Reduced anxiety about being seen entering a therapy office or running into people you know.

Consistent Support

Maintain regular therapy sessions even during illness, bad weather, or other circumstances that might prevent in-person attendance, ensuring continuity of care.

Technology Integration

Use of secure, encrypted platforms that comply with NHS data protection standards, ensuring your privacy and confidentiality are completely protected throughout the process.

Specialised Support Areas in Individual Therapy

Anxiety Disorders and Stress Management

Anxiety affects millions of adults across the UK, manifesting in various forms from generalised anxiety disorder and panic attacks to social anxiety and specific phobias. In individual therapy, we work together to understand your unique anxiety triggers, develop effective coping strategies, and gradually build your confidence in managing anxious thoughts and feelings.

Through techniques such as cognitive restructuring, exposure therapy, mindfulness practices, and relaxation training, many clients experience significant reduction in anxiety symptoms and improved quality of life. We also explore the underlying beliefs and experiences that may contribute to your anxiety, addressing root causes rather than just symptoms.

Depression and Mood Disorder Treatment

Depression can feel isolating and overwhelming, affecting every aspect of your life from relationships and work to physical health and self-perception. Individual therapy provides a safe space to explore the complex factors contributing to your depression whilst developing practical strategies for managing difficult periods and building resilience.

We work together to identify negative thought patterns, explore past experiences that may influence current mood, and develop behavioural strategies that can help lift depression. Many clients find that understanding their depression better helps reduce self-criticism and builds hope for recovery.

Trauma Recovery and PTSD Support

Trauma can have lasting impacts on mental health, relationships, and daily functioning. Whether you've experienced a single traumatic event or ongoing trauma, individual therapy provides a safe environment to process these experiences at your own pace. I use trauma-informed approaches that prioritise your safety and empowerment throughout the healing process.

Trauma therapy focuses on helping your nervous system process traumatic memories safely, developing healthy coping mechanisms, and reclaiming your sense of personal power and control. Recovery is possible, and many trauma survivors find that therapy helps them not just survive but thrive.

Relationship Difficulties and Communication Patterns

Individual therapy can be incredibly helpful for addressing relationship issues, even when you're attending alone. We explore your relationship patterns, communication styles, boundary-setting abilities, and how past experiences influence current relationships. Many clients find that working on themselves individually leads to significant improvements in their relationships with partners, family members, friends, and colleagues.

Self-Esteem and Personal Development

In individual therapy, we work to understand how your self-perception developed, challenge negative self-beliefs, and build a more balanced, compassionate relationship with yourself. This work often has positive ripple effects across all areas of life.

The Journey of Personal Growth Through Therapy

Building Self-Awareness and Insight

One of the most valuable aspects of individual therapy is developing deeper self-awareness. Through our therapeutic conversations, you'll begin to notice patterns in your thoughts, feelings, and behaviours that you may not have recognised before. This awareness becomes the foundation for meaningful change.

Many clients describe therapy as providing them with a clearer understanding of themselves - their triggers, strengths, values, and goals. This self-knowledge empowers you to make conscious choices about how you want to live your life rather than simply reacting to circumstances.

Developing Healthy Coping Strategies

Life will always present challenges, but therapy helps you develop a toolkit of healthy coping strategies to manage difficult situations more effectively. These might include stress management techniques, communication skills, boundary-setting abilities, emotional regulation strategies, and problem-solving approaches.

The coping strategies we develop together are tailored to your specific needs, lifestyle, and preferences. What works for one person may not work for another, so we collaborate to find approaches that feel authentic and sustainable for you.
